Color Permutation

Jonas Probst, 2012

A program computing all permutations of colors within the RGB color space in a area of a given resolution. Thus showing all possible digital images of the past, present and future within these boundaries.
Due to the magnitude of imagery and limited by the ability of computers today, this process takes billions of years to finish.

  • Resolution: 256 x 192 px
  • Duration: 5 x (255 x 255 x 255)^(256 x 192) ms
